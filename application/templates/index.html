Welcome to Vicipaedia Arcanum!

Vicipaedia Arcanum (from now on referred to as VA) is an encyclopedia for tabletop RPG games. With VA, you and your party can track creatures and NPCs you have encountered during your travels.

To get started, first create an account. Then you can either create a new campaign or join an existing one.
